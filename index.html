Business Problem

Data Professional Survey Workforce Analytics Dashboard

The HR and talent acquisition team needed to understand the data job market who data professionals are, what they earn, and how satisfied they are with their careers. Built a 5 page interactive dashboard analyzing 630 survey respondents.

Key Findings
  • 59% of data professionals switched from a different career
  • Salary satisfaction scored 4.27/10 the lowest of all dimensions
  • Python dominates every single job title in the field
  • Data Scientists earn 71% more than the average Data Analyst

FAAC Federal Revenue Analysis, 5 Year Nigeria Fiscal Dashboard

An investor facing analysis of Nigeria's federal revenue distribution from 2020 to 2024. Built from 61 raw government Excel files sourced from the NBS. Covers oil dependency, state-level exposure, revenue mix shifts, and the impact of the 2023 Naira devaluation.

Business Questions
